CEC Robotics

Our team, FTC #3527, is a self funded team from Louisville, KY. We represent CEC, Christian Educational Consortium, and have operated for two years. Our team is the second robotics team CEC has had. We replaced the previous team, after it was disbanded and was inactive for two years. At the Kentucky FTC Tournament in 2015, we recieved the Rockwell Collins Design award for our outstanding robot design. We were also the runner-up alliance, and we received the second place Inspire award.


We use COTS

In creating our forklift and sweeper, we decided to use 80/20 instead of standard TETRIX materials. This maximizes efficiency and minimizes resistance in the forklift.

Powered by

Instead of settling for the status quo in CAD, we took it to the next level and used the industry standard SolidWorks. We believe that in using Solidworks we are preparing our members for careers in engineering, because it is becoming the preferred 3D design software for many companies.


Open Source

The website code, the robot code, and the robot design files are openly available on our public FTP repo, so that we are not only helping our team, we are helping any future team that needs code.
